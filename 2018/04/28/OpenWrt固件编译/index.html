编译,刷机

$ scp bin/targets/ramips/mt7628/lede-ramips-mt7628-hc5661a-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin root@192.168.199.1:/tmp/
$ ssh root@192.168.199.1
$ mtd write /tmp/lede-ramips-mt7628-hc5661a-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin firmware
reboot

总结

烧写好固件,路由器的网关为192.168.1.1 , 密码:无. 用此固件测试联想的newifi y1 路由器会出现2.4G WIFI 能连接上但是无法上网的问题.相关论坛 , 优酷路由宝相关,openwrt.io

错误 ieee80211 phy3: rt2x00queue_write_tx_frame: Error - Dropping frame due to full tx queue 2

mt7620 cc openwrt 15.05.1 无线客户端多了报错:ieee80211 phy0: rt2x00queue_write_tx_frame: Error – Dropping frame due to full tx queue 2的处理办法：
/openwrt/build_dir/target-mipsel_24kec+dsp_uClibc-0.9.33.2/linux-ramips_mt7620/linux-3.18.xx/drivers/net/wireless/ralink/rt2x00/rt2800mmio.c文件里
修改:case QID_AC_BK:
– queue->limit = 64;
+ queue->limit = 128;
